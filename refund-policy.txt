REFUND POLICY

Last updated: January 2024

1. OVERVIEW

At Rycene VLSI, we are committed to providing high-quality educational content and ensuring student satisfaction. This refund policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be requested and processed.

2. REFUND ELIGIBILITY

2.1 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee
- Refunds may be requested within 7 days of course purchase
- You must have completed less than 30% of the course content
- The refund request must include a valid reason
- This guarantee applies to individual course purchases only

2.2 Conditions for Refund Eligibility
- Course access was not significantly used (less than 30% completion)
- No course materials were downloaded or printed
- No certificates were issued
- Request is made within the specified time frame

3. NON-REFUNDABLE SITUATIONS

Refunds will NOT be provided in the following cases:
- Requests made after the 7-day period
- Course completion exceeds 30% of total content
- Course materials have been downloaded or shared
- Certificate of completion has been issued
- Violation of our terms of service
- Technical issues on the student's end (internet connectivity, device compatibility)
- Change of mind after significant course consumption

4. REFUND PROCESS

4.1 How to Request a Refund
- Send an email to vlsieasyteam@gmail.com
- Include your order number and course details
- Provide a clear reason for the refund request
- Allow 3-5 business days for initial review

4.2 Refund Processing
- Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days
- Refunds will be credited to the original payment method
- Processing time may vary depending on your bank or payment provider
- You will receive email confirmation once the refund is processed

5. PARTIAL REFUNDS

In exceptional circumstances, partial refunds may be considered:
- Technical issues that significantly impact course delivery
- Course content that materially differs from the description
- Instructor unavailability for extended periods
- Each case will be reviewed individually

6. COURSE BUNDLE REFUNDS

For course bundles or packages:
- Refund eligibility applies to the entire bundle
- Individual courses within a bundle cannot be refunded separately
- The 30% completion rule applies to the overall bundle progress
- All courses in the bundle must meet refund criteria

7. PROMOTIONAL AND DISCOUNTED COURSES

- Courses purchased with promotional codes or discounts are eligible for refunds
- Refund amount will be based on the actual amount paid
- Special promotional terms may apply to certain offers

8. TECHNICAL ISSUES

8.1 Platform-Related Issues
- If technical problems on our end prevent course access, full refunds may be provided
- We will first attempt to resolve technical issues before processing refunds
- Temporary technical difficulties do not qualify for automatic refunds

8.2 Student-Side Technical Issues
- Issues related to student's internet connection, device compatibility, or software are not grounds for refunds
- We provide technical support to help resolve such issues
- System requirements are clearly stated for each course

9. REFUND EXCEPTIONS

9.1 Emergency Situations
- Medical emergencies or family crises may be considered for refund exceptions
- Documentation may be required to verify exceptional circumstances
- Each case will be reviewed with compassion and flexibility

9.2 Course Quality Issues
- If course content has significant quality issues, refunds may be provided beyond the standard period
- We encourage students to report quality concerns immediately
- We continuously work to improve course content based on feedback

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

10.1 Internal Resolution
- We encourage students to contact us directly for refund discussions
- Our support team will work to find mutually acceptable solutions
- Most refund requests are resolved within 5-7 business days

10.2 External Dispute Resolution
- If internal resolution is not satisfactory, students may contact their payment provider
- Chargeback disputes will be handled according to payment processor guidelines
- We maintain detailed records of all transactions and course access

11. REFUND POLICY CHANGES

- This refund policy may be updated from time to time
- Changes will be posted on our website with the updated date
- Existing enrollments will be governed by the policy in effect at the time of purchase

12. CONTACT INFORMATION

For refund requests or questions about this policy:
- Email: vlsieasyteam@gmail.com
- Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Course Name]"
- Include: Order number, course details, and reason for refund

13. IMPORTANT NOTES

- Refunds are processed in the same currency as the original purchase
- International transaction fees are not refundable
- We reserve the right to refuse refunds for suspected fraudulent activity
- This policy complies with applicable consumer protection laws

14. SATISFACTION GUARANTEE

While we offer this refund policy, our primary goal is student success and satisfaction. We encourage students to:
- Review course previews and descriptions carefully before purchasing
- Contact support if you have questions about course content
- Utilize our student support resources for technical assistance
- Provide feedback to help us improve our courses

We believe in the quality of our education and are committed to helping every student succeed in their VLSI learning journey.
